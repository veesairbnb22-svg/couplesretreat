The Intent

Why should you attend Couples Retreat?

Marriage is a journey, and every journey requires moments of rest, reflection, and renewal. The Couples Retreat is a three-day, quarterly experience intentionally created to help couples heal, reconnect, and grow stronger together.

This retreat provides a safe and supportive environment where couples can confront unresolved issues, heal emotional wounds, and receive answers to long-held questions that often go unspoken in marriages.
